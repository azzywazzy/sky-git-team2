About Us

Welcome to the Furry Godmother Vet Clinic with over 30 years of experience in providing compassionate care to pets. Our team of experienced veterinary surgeons and staff are dedicated to delivering personalized care to each and every animal that walks through our doors.

With a focus on personalized care, we understand that every pet is unique and requires individual attention and treatment.

Our commitment to excellence is evident in every aspect of our practice, from routine check-ups to complex surgeries. As a trusted and established practice in Leeds, we are proud to have earned a reputation for providing exceptional care to our patients and their owners.
